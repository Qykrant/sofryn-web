Smoothfy

Polished GUI open & close animations for every inventory and container screen in Minecraft.

◆ Forge  |  1.20.1  &  1.20.2
  • Overview
  • License

What it does

Adds smooth scale animations (open & close) to all container GUIs — inventory, chest, crafting table, furnace, and any modded container screen.

Author

TheKrant

Supported versions

Minecraft 1.20.1 (Forge 47.4.10+)
Minecraft 1.20.2 (Forge 48.1.0+)

License

SmoothFy Custom License 1.0 — Read it here

How it works

Uses Forge mixins and the ScreenEvent render pipeline to apply a PoseStack scale transform on every container screen frame. The animation runs at 200 ms with a quadratic ease-out on open and ease-in on close. A hasRenderedOnce guard prevents false close animations (e.g. the creative inventory double-open quirk in vanilla Forge).
